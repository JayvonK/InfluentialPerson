INFLUENCE

Whenever I think about a person who’s had and influence on my life, I think of the great Kobe Bryant. Why Kobe? Well the main reason why Kobe has had such an influence on my life is because of his worth ethic, and his mamba mentality. Kobe is the one who showed me that working hard pays off, and that there are no excuses. If you put your mind to it, you can achieve anything. Also Kobe is one of the main reasons why I still keep playing basketball, and why I don’t ever give up without at least trying. Whenever I’m feeling a certain way, like distraught, down, upset or angry, basketball never fails to calm me down and set me straight. I want to thank Kobe for that.
R.I.P Kobe Bryant
